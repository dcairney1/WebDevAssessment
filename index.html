EDINBURGH

Scotland's cultural capital


Edinburgh is the capital of Scotland and it is located in central eastern Scotland, near the Firth of Forth, close to the North Sea. Thanks to the spectacular rocks, rustic buildings and a huge collection of medieval and classic architecture, including numerous stone decorations, it is one of the most lively cities in Europe.


It is affectionatly known to Scottish people as Auld Reekie, Edina, Athens of the North and Britain’s Other Eye. With the city’s skyline, cobbled streets and colourful characters as your backdrop, visit Edinburgh and create memories that will last a lifetime. Are you ready for more? There’s so many ways to embrace the city, explore, take a look around the website for more information about the city.
